Pastor Tacos

Description

Pastor tacos are a delicious and savory taco that originated in Puebla, Mexico. They are made with pork, pineapple, and onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork
  • 1 cup pineapple
  • 1/2 cup onions

Instructions

  1. Cook the pork
  2. Add pineapple to the pork
  3. Add onions to the pork
  4. Place pork, pineapple, and onion mixture in tortillas

Enjoy!
