Quesabirria Tacos

Description

Quesabirria tacos are a delicious and cheesy taco that originated in Tijuana, Mexico. They are made with beef, cheese, and corn tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef
  • 1 cup cheese
  • 10 corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Add cheese to the beef
  3. Place beef and cheese mixture in tortillas
  4. Cook the tacos

Enjoy!
